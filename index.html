Engineering Mechanics Notes

1. Force

Force is a push or pull acting on a body. It can change the motion or shape of an object.

Unit: Newton (N)

2. Types of Forces

  • Contact Force
  • Non-Contact Force
  • Gravitational Force
  • Frictional Force

3. Moment of Force

The turning effect of a force about a point is called moment.

Formula: Moment = Force × Distance

4. Equilibrium

A body is said to be in equilibrium when the sum of all forces acting on it is zero.

5. Friction

Friction is the resistance between two surfaces in contact.
